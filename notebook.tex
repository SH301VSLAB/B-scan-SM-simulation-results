\paragraph{Experimental Setup:}\label{experimental-setup}}

The experimental setup is as shown below:

The parameters used are as per the tutorial in
\href{https://support.onscale.com/hc/en-us/articles/360022005111-Simulating-Wave-Propagation-in-a-Steel-Plate-with-a-Defect}{link}

As can be seen from the tutorial time domain signal is obtained at
X=20mm and Y=0mm but for this work X has been chosen to be X=40.5mm(end
of the plate) and Y varying from 0 to 22.5 mm.

Note 1: * Here X is the horizontal axis and Y is the vertical axis

Note 2: * The Y position of receiver has been changed in increments of
0.2mm
